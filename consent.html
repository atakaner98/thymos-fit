EXPLICIT CONSENT

Cloud Sync
Explicit Consent Text

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This text explains the optional explicit consent requested before enabling THYMOS cloud sync. For general privacy information, see our Privacy Policy and KVKK Aydınlatma Metni.

1. Short Consent Text

"By enabling optional cloud sync, I give my explicit consent to the processing of my workout data, templates and programs, height/weight information, account email used for sync, and technical sync identifiers by data controller Atakan ER for the purpose of backup and synchronization across my own devices. I understand that exercise notes remain on my device only and are not transmitted to the server. I understand that I can withdraw this consent at any time through in-app account deletion (Settings > Sync > Delete Account) or by contacting support."

2. Full Consent Text

THYMOS can be used offline and without an account. Cloud sync is an optional feature.

If you choose to enable cloud sync, the following data categories may be processed for backup and synchronization across your devices:

  • workout history,
  • exercise, set, rep, duration, load and similar training data,
  • templates, programs and schedule records,
  • user preferences such as favorites, rest timer and unit settings; exercise notes are excluded and remain only on your device,
  • height and body weight information,
  • account email used for synchronization,
  • device identifiers, principal/alias records, timestamps and technical sync metadata.

This explicit consent:

  • applies only to cloud synchronization, backup and cross-device sync,
  • is not used for marketing, advertising or behavioral tracking,
  • is not a precondition for using the app offline.

If you do not consent:

  • you may continue using THYMOS offline on your device,
  • but you may not use cloud sync, account linking or cross-device backup features.

You may withdraw your consent at any time through the in-app account deletion flow (Settings > Sync > Delete Account) or by contacting support. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal. After withdrawal, cloud sync stops and synced server data is permanently deleted, while minimal consent records may be retained where legally required.

Infrastructure providers and transfer details relating to cloud sync are described separately in the KVKK Aydınlatma Metni and Privacy Policy.
