🔒 DATA PRIVACY

Your fitness data can identify you
among 100 million people in 6 days.
Here's what we do about it.

Workout logs aren't just numbers — they're a biometric fingerprint of your body, habits, and schedule. Most fitness apps treat that data as a product. THYMOS treats it as yours. Everything stays on your device by default. No behavioral tracking. No third-party data sharing. Sensitive fields — like readiness scores and wellness context — never leave your phone, ever.

📱
On-Device First
Your data lives on your device. Cloud backup is optional — your phone always stays in control.
🚫
Zero Tracking
No behavioral analytics, no ad SDKs, no third-party data brokers. Nothing watching you lift.
🔐
Sensitive Data Stays Local
Readiness scores, wellness context, and RPE signals are marked sensitive and never synced to the cloud.
📤
Full Export, Anytime
Export everything as CSV or JSON whenever you want. No lock-in. No asking permission.
